I received the B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from the University of Isfahan, in 2009, the M.Sc. degree in computer science from Saarland University, in 2012, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering from Carleton University, in 2018. During my master’s studies, I was with IMPRS, Max Planck Institute for Informatics. During my Ph.D. studies, I was a member of the Huawei-Carleton collaborative research project. I am currently with the Huawei Ottawa Research Centre.

My experience ranges from communication systems design, analysis, and programming to circuits implementation. My research interests include coding and information theory, digital communications, and signal processing.
